Pasta Carbonara Recipe

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper for a creamy, delicious flavor.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 25 minutes
  • Preparation: 10 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta (or bacon)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 clove garlic
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water before draining.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and Parmesan cheese together.
  3. Fry the pancetta in a pan with a little oil until crispy.
  4. Add the drained pasta to the pan and mix well with the pancetta.
  5. Remove the pan from heat, then quickly stir in the egg mixture and a splash of pasta water to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Season with black pepper and serve immediately with extra Parmesan.
